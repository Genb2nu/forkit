What is ForkIt?

Recipes were boring.
We fixed that.

Most recipe sites are encyclopedias nobody asked for. You open one, get hit with a 3,000-word essay about someone's grandmother, scroll past 47 ads, and still can't tell if you actually want to cook it.

ForkIt is built differently. Swipe through recipe cards like you scroll music — fast, tactile, fun. Every card shows you country, cook time, difficulty, and creator upfront. Decide in a second.

When you find something worth making? Full ingredient checklist, step-by-step instructions, and an embedded video — all right there. No scrolling back to the top. No hunting for the recipe buried under a life story.

And the best part? You don't need an account to start. Browse everything, swipe everything, watch every video — completely free, no sign-up required. Create an account only when you want to save recipes or share your own.

💰 Monetization Roadmap — Coming as We Grow

We're building the platform first, the revenue model second. Here's the honest plan for how creators will earn real money once ForkIt has the audience to make it meaningful.

STAGE 1 · NOW
Recognition Economy
Tier badges, SEO portfolio, social shoutouts, leaderboard status. Real value, zero cash.
STAGE 2 · YEAR 1
Creator Tipping
Optional "buy me a coffee" on creator profiles via Stripe. ForkIt takes 0% — you keep it all.
STAGE 3 · YEAR 2
Sponsored Placements
Brands pay ForkIt for featured slots. 30% of that placement fee goes back to the recipe creator.
STAGE 4 · YEAR 2+
Creator Fund
Platform revenue pooled monthly. Distributed to top creators by vote-weighted formula. Transparent. Public.
