3 · Animated Cooking Steps

Each step now has a cooking micro-animation showing the action (cutting, sautéing, simmering, etc.), a timer badge, and optional pro tips.

0/6 done
1
Prep the Ingredients

Crush garlic cloves. Slice onion into rings. Cut chicken into serving pieces. Pat dry with paper towels.

🔪 🧄 🧅 🍗
💡 Tip: Crushing garlic (not mincing) releases more flavor and holds up better during braising.
2
Sear the Chicken

Heat oil in a pan over high heat. Sear chicken pieces skin-side down until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.

🍳 💨 💨 💨
6 min
💡 Tip: Don't move the chicken! Let it develop a golden crust before flipping.
3
Sauté Garlic & Onion

Remove chicken, set aside. In the same pan, sauté crushed garlic and onion rings until fragrant and translucent.

🔪 🧄 🧅 → 🍳
3 min
4
Combine the Braising Liquid

Return chicken to pan. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and water. Add bay leaves and peppercorns. Do NOT stir yet.

🍗 🫗 🫗 🍶 🌿 ⚫
💡 Tip: Don't stir after adding vinegar — it can become bitter if agitated early. Let it come to a boil first.
5
Simmer Until Tender

Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for 30-35 minutes until chicken is fork-tender and sauce thickens.

🫕 💧 💧 💧 💧
30-35 min
6
Plate & Serve

Transfer chicken to a plate. Spoon the rich adobo sauce over the top. Serve with steamed rice. Enjoy! 🍚
